ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is HANS, the founder of hansCharmingFoam and the coolest researcher in the energy institute in Sheffield, UK.
My research direction is to solve the problem of vertical axis wind turbine airfoil optimisation in the form of CFD simulation based on OpenFOAM, and develop wind turbine airfoil design and transformation tools, aiming to provide optimal engineering design to address the self-starting and efficiency problems.
Here at hansCharmingFoam, you can get a step-to-step airfoil aerodynamic OpenFOAM simulation visualised toturials. It can provide the most comprehensive case files and dynamic explanation of each step. The airfoil resources encountered in the simulation and the powerful online transcoder can quickly achieve the desired airfoil design.
